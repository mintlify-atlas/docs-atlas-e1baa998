Modern Tooltips replace the classic Ultima Online tooltip system with a fully browser-rendered alternative. Instead of the plain text boxes the original client produces, tooltips are rendered natively by the browser with improved layout, legibility, and interactive features like equipment comparisons and colour-coded rarity indicators.

<Note>
  Enable **Modern Tooltips** under **Experimental** in [Game Options](/features/game-options) to use this feature.
</Note>

## Highlights

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sleek Interface" icon="eye">
    Cleaner layout with better readability. Numbers are left-aligned and text is formatted for quick scanning.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Item Icons" icon="image">
    Item and equipment icons are shown directly in the tooltip alongside the property list.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Equipment Comparisons" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Hold **Ctrl** while hovering an item to see a comparison against your currently equipped item in the same slot, showing expected stat changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rarity Colouring" icon="palette">
    If your shard sends a colour for items (e.g. UOAlive magic item colours), the tooltip background and text will reflect the item's rarity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Positive/Negative Highlighting" icon="circle-half-stroke">
    Property modifiers are colour-coded — positive values appear in one colour and negative in another for instant visual feedback.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Durability Meter" icon="battery-half">
    Items with durability show a visual meter indicating current condition at a glance.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Enabling Modern Tooltips

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Game Options">
    Press `Alt+O` or click the gear icon to open the Options window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Experimental">
    Navigate to the **Experimental** tab in the Options window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Modern Tooltips">
    Toggle **Modern Tooltips** on. The setting takes effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For the best experience, also enable **Modern Grid Containers** alongside Modern Tooltips. Grid containers display tooltips inline when hovering items in a container, making it easy to evaluate loot without opening each item individually.
</Tip>
